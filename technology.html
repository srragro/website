02 Key technical advantages

Ideal for decentralized waste management in India — offering superior control and adaptability.

i.

Feedstock Flexibility

Processes a wide range of biomass including cotton stalks, mustard straw, and municipal waste with minimal pre-processing.

Cotton Stalks Mustard Straw Coconut Shells Sugarcane Residue Municipal Biomass

Minimal pre-processing — the chamber takes the feedstock as the farm delivers it.

ii.

Precision Control

Accurate control of temperature and dwell time for high-quality biochar.

iii.

Energy Self-Sufficiency

Syngas released during carbonization is redirected back into the furnace, making the process nearly self-sustaining.

A closed thermal loop — the kind of detail that survives a Life Cycle Assessment.
